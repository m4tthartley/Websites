State of Decay

by Matt on 21 Oct 2013

I have recently been playing State of Decay a lot and it’s some of the most fun I’ve ever had in single player. It’s everything you would want from a zombie survival game. The only downside is that the story is quite short and I just want more cause it was so fun!
